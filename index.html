How it works

Simple. Invisible.
Actually effective.

👁

Lives in your menu bar

No Dock icon, no clutter. Just a quiet countdown in your menu bar — always visible, never intrusive.

🚀

Auto-starts on login

Install once, forget about it. Eye Timer launches silently every time you log in and starts counting immediately.

🔒

Screen lock aware

Lock your Mac and the timer pauses. Unlock and it resets to a fresh 20 minutes — because your eyes were already resting.

🔔

Native notifications

macOS alerts — not browser popups. A gentle nudge when it's time to look away, with a rotating tip each time.

🌿

20-second rest timer

Switches to a rest countdown automatically. Look 20 feet away, breathe, blink — then back to work.

📊

Daily break tracker

Click the menu bar icon to see how many breaks you've taken today. Small wins, big difference.
